Motivation

Understanding Global Trade is Hard. Doesn't have to be

In the last month, US has announced tariffs on products coming from China. While the US and the World at large have recoiled and protested the new tariffs, it has brought to the light a lack of knowledge about the global economy.

It’s very difficult to track the resources that flow in and out of nations in this globalized modern world. Our visualization aims to utilize many data sources to provide a visual representation of the inter-woven nature of international industries. Our hope is to show the interdependence of nations. In addition, our hope is to raise awareness for other countries and their industries.
